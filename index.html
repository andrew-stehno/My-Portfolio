About

I am a Full Stack Web Developer with a background in communication and training, which allows me to better understand the needs of customers, and to coordinate tasks within a group setting. I’ve earned a certificate in Full Stack Development from the University of Utah, with skills in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, NodeJs, React and responsive web design. Having Spent my entire career working directly with high-value customers and peers, I’m confident that I can bring my interpersonal and leadership skills to the table, along with my technical know-how, to be a part of a cohesive, results-oriented team.


Most recently, as a Project Manager on a team of three, I was able to oversee the development of a health-conscious webpage, that allows users to search and save recipes, view ingredients, create shopping lists, and log personal health information. I’m excited to continue making life enabling web content, to continue learning, and to be a part of a meaningful organization, where I can use my expertise to solve tomorrow’s problems, today.
